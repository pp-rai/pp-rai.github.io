program

The conference will consist of the following research tracks:

  • — NLP+ASR+CAI (natural language processing, automatic speech recognition, and conversational AI)
  • — CV (computer vision)
  • — KE (knowledge engineering)
  • — ML (machine learning)
  • — PS+O (problem solving and optimization)
  • — UAI (uncertainty in artificial intelligence)
  • — NI (neuroinformatics)
  • — R (robotics)
  • — Young.AI (session for young researchers)
  • — General.AI (another AI's directions)
